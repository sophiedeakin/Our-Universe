Space Museum Exhibits

Astronaut Exhibit

Become an astronaut for the day by visiting the Astronaut Exhibit. There is so much to discover in this exhibit from replica astronaut space suits you can try on and learning all about the famous astronauts that went into space and their stories.

Fun Fact: Did you know astronauts can grow up to 3% taller while spending time in microgravity? When astronauts return to Earth, their height returns to normal after a few months.

Satellites Exhibit

Have you ever wondered what satellites are in space and what's their job? In the Satellite Exhibit you can discover the Satellites that are in space with the Satellite Tracker. We are the only space museum in the world to have a Satellite Tracker that allows visitors to see and track the positions of the Satellites and learn what they do.

Fun Fact: Did you know Satellites travel at 18,000 miles per hour? That means in a given day, Satellites can travel the entire circumference of the Earth about 14 times.

VR Space Room Exhibit

Experience the brand new VR Space Room Exhibit. Visitors can experience space like never before, with the VR Space Room provides outstanding visuals and sound effects. Why not try out the VR Space Rocket, strap in and blast off into space.

Fun Fact: Did you know for years it was believed that Earth was the only planet in our solar system with liquid water? More recently, NASA revealed its strongest evidence yet that there is intermittent running water on Mars, too.

Planetarium Exhibit

In the Planetarium Exhibit, visitors can sit back and relax in our 4k theatre dome which allows visitors to fully experience and explore outer space, astronomy and stargazing. Images appear all around you, which makes you feel completely immersed.

Fun Fact: Did you know an asteroid about the size of a car enters Earth's atmosphere roughly once a year - but it burns up before it reaches us? Phew!
