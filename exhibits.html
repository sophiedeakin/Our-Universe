Space Museum Exhibits

Astronaut Exhibit

Become an astronaut for the day by visiting the Astronaut Exhibit, there so much to discover in this exhibit from replica astronaut space suits that you can try on and learn all about the famous astronaut that went into space and thier stories.

Fun Fact: Did you know astronauts can grow up to 3% taller while spending yime in microgravity. When astronauts return to earth, their height returns to normal after a few months.

Satallite Exhibit

Have you ever wondered what statllites are in space and what's their job? In the Satallite Exhibit you can discover the satallites that are in space with the Satallite Tracker. We are the only space museum in the world to have a Satallite Tracker that allows visitors to see track the postitions of the satallites and learn what they do.

Fun Fact: Did you know satallites travel at 18,000 miles per hour. That means in a given day, satallites can travel the entire circumference of the Earth about 14 times.

VR Space Room Exhibit

Experience the brand new VR Space Room Exhibit. Visitors can experience space like never before, the VR Space Room provides outstanding visuals and sound effects. Why not try out the VR Space Rocket, strap in and blast off into space.

Fun Fact: Did you know for years it was believed that Earth was the only planet in our solar system with liquid water. More recently, NASA revealed its strongest evidence yet that there is intermittent running water on Mars, too.

Planetarium Exhibit

In the Planetarium Exhibit visitors can sit back and relax in our 4k theatre dome which is designed to allow visitors to fully experience and explore outer space, atronomy and stargazing. Images appear all around you which makes you feel completely immersed.

Fun Fact: Did you know an asteriod about the size of a car enters Earth's atmosphere roughly once a year - but it burns up before it reaches us. Phew!
